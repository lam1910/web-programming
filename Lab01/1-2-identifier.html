National Identification Number
is used by the governments of many countries as a means of tracking their citizens, permanent residents, and temporary residents for the purposes of work, taxation, government benefits, health care, and other governmentally-related functions
Passport Number
the number on the passport of one person. It is uniquely identified a single person from that country. Used when traveling to another country. Can be use inside the country issuing the passport as a form of identification
  • Student Id
    • issued by school to student to uniquely identified a single student. Common form included: Initial of school (for national-wide system), initial of faculty of the student, class of student (class of 2016) and 4-6 alphanumerical values to distinguished between students.
  • International Geo Sample Number
    • is a sample identification code of typically nine characters. Example: SSH000SUA
  • Flight number
    • is a code for an airline service consisting of two-character airline designator and a 1 to 4 digit number. Used to distinguished flights on the sky or airport.
